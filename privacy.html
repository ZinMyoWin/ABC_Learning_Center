ABC Learning Privacy Policy

ABC Learning (also referred to as "we" or "us" in this Privacy Policy) values the confidentiality and protection of your information. We operate the ABC Learning website, which includes content and pages located within the abc-learning.com web domain and ABC Learning mobile applications.

The ABC Learning website allows you to:

  • Browse courses and programs offered by ABC Learning and its partners
  • Create an ABC Learning account
  • Sign up to receive more information about courses and programs

This Privacy Policy explains how ABC Learning collects, uses, and shares your Personal Information when you interact with the website or when someone interacts with the site on your behalf (such as an administrator for the institution sponsoring your enrollment). "Personal Information" refers to information that can directly or indirectly identify an individual.

This Privacy Policy also describes how ABC Learning uses your Personal Information when you interact with us on other websites, such as social media sites.

If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access, browse, or create an account on the ABC Learning website, enroll in any courses, or complete any form requesting additional information about offerings.

Please note that this Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time, and the most recent version will be posted on our website.
