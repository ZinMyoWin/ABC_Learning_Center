Microsoft Office

This course covers the Microsoft Office suite, including Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and Outlook. Students will learn how to use each application effectively and efficiently, including formatting documents, creating spreadsheets, and designing presentations. The course is taught by Mr.Kevin Hart and starts on Saturdays from 10 am to 1 pm. It takes 6 weeks to complete the course.
The course outcomes include proficiency in using Microsoft Office applications for personal and professional use.
